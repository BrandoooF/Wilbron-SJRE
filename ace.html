A NEW GENERATION OF CIVIC LEADERSHIP

WHAT IS ACE?

As the mayor's largest community engagement initiative, the Academy of Civic Engagement (ACE) is a city-wide leadership program from the Mayor's Office aimed at getting residents directly involved and engaged with their local municpal government. ACE brings everyday residents together for a deeper understanding of how the City of Birmingham works for them and how they can connect to a variety of engagement opportunites. The central goal of ACE is to prepare residents to go out in their communities to act, serve and lead the City of Birmingham towards a brighter future for all.

WHO CAN APPLY?

Are you a student (age 16+)? Do you currently work within the City of Birmingham? Are you simply passionate about serving others and the City of Birmingham? If the answer is yes to any of these questions, and you are currently a resident of the City of Birmingham, then you should apply to be a part of ACE.

WHAT'S IN IT FOR YOU?

The City of Birmingham is changing at a fast pace. But, those changes only matter when they serve the people first and when they are led by the residents foremost. ACE wants you to be at the center of this change and provides all residents with an opportunity to engage directly with City leadership. Here's what you need to know:

ACE is a seven-week program. The program will feature dynamic sessions covering the following topics:

  • Birmingham Past, Present and Future
  • Public Safety Strategies and Updates
  • Understanding Birmingham's Infrastructure
  • Serving Birmingham: Path to Leadership
  • Neighborhood Revitalization & opportunity for All

ACE gives you a behind-the-scenes look into what it takes to run the city and how you can help. Program participants recieve access to in-person learning sessions. Speakers from various departments and divisions will share insight into how their area operates.
