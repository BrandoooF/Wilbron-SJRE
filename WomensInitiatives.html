These women will work to solve issues through research, policy/advocacy and community engagement. By focusing on women, the Division of Social Justice and Racial Equity aims to focus on issues impacting women and girls in our area. We are commited to creating pathways and enhancing the quality of life and justice for all women and girls. The involvement of men and non-binary people is vital to this work as they are essential to naming and addressing the harms that impact women and girls in our community.
