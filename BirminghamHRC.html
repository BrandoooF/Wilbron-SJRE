WELCOME

The Human Rights Commission (HRC) is responsible for investigating,advising and reporting to the Office of the Mayor and each City Council member on matters of resolving discriminatory practices,including potential legislative or administrative actions to eliminate discrimination. There are 15 members and a president of the commission who preside over the meetings along with the city clerk and a city attorney.
